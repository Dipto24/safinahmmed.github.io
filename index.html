I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of CSE at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), where I work on predictable, trustworthy AI-enabled systems — and a prospective PhD applicant in Computer Science.

My Research:

Real-Time & Cyber-Physical Systems
scheduling-aware ROS 2 executors for predictable robotic execution, and response-time analysis of mixed timer–subscription workloads
Trustworthy & Privacy-Preserving AI
privacy-preserving similarity learning for motion-based XR identification, resilient to attribute inference, membership inference, and linkability attacks
Medical AI & Computer Vision
skin lesion analysis, melanoma detection across skin tones, breast ultrasound segmentation
Physiological Signal Analysis
cuffless blood pressure estimation from PPG signals
